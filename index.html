About Us

Siva waterproofing is into waterproofing coating business in chennai. We specialize in protective coating, Pressure Grounding, Structural repairs and rehabilitation. We have a vast range of proven protective coatings that are suitable for all surface typees. Our coatings are high performance 100% solid speciality coatings designed to protect various industrial structures, equipments and civil structures against corrison, heat and chemical attack, fungus, ultraviolet radiation. Empowered with creative skills, impeccable workmanship and efficient service, Rithka Alcheme team has successfully created its stamp of elegance and efficiency on many large corporate offices, hotels, restaurants, cafes, clubs, showrooms and residential Buildings.
