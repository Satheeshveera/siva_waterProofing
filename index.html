About Us

Siva waterproofing is into waterproofing coating business in chennai. We specialize in protective coating, Pressure Grounding, Structural repairs and rehabilitation. Our coatings are high performance 100% solid speciality coatings designed to protect various industrial structures, equipments and civil structures against corrison, heat and chemical attack, fungus, ultraviolet radiation.
